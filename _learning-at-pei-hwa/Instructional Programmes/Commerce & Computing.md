<h6 style="color:#0B6623;font-family:sans-serif;font-weight:bold;margin-top:30px;"><strong style="font-family:sans-serif;font-size:17px;color:#0B6623;">Philosophy </strong></h6>
	
<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2;font-family:sans-serif;margin-top:0px;"><i style="font-size:14.5px;font-family:sans-serif;color: inherit;">“To develop future-ready youths who are critical thinkers with an enterprising mindset, through an engaging and relevant Commerce &amp; Computing curriculum”</i></p>
<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2;font-family:sans-serif;margin-top:0px;">The Commerce &amp; Computing department equips students with a holistic understanding of business principles and digital technologies, fostering critical thinking and ethical decision-making to be applicable in the dynamic world of Commerce and  Computing. </p>


<h6 style="color:#0B6623;font-family:sans-serif;font-weight:bold;margin-top:30px;"><strong style="font-family:sans-serif;font-size:17px;color:#0B6623;">Curriculum</strong></h6>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:1.8;margin-top:0px;font-family:sans-serif;">Our mission is to cultivate the minds of students, equipping them with essential skills that bridge the realms of computational thinking and critical analysis. Our dedicated team is committed to nurturing students into adept problem solvers and proficient decision-makers, paving the way for their success in an ever-evolving digital landscape.</p>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2;font-family:sans-serif;margin-top:0px;">As the students embark on this educational journey with us, our interactive and captivating lessons act as vehicles for learning. Through the artful integration of questioning techniques, students are encouraged to explore concepts deeply, fostering a culture of curiosity and exploration. Moreover, our lessons are enriched with real-world scenario-based examples, providing practical contexts that enhance their understanding and application of theoretical knowledge.
</p>
