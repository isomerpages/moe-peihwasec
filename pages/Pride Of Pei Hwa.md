<h6 style="color:#0B6623;font-family:sans-serif;font-weight:bold;margin-top:30px;"><i><strong style="font-family:sans-serif;font-size:17px;color:#0B6623;">Our Teachers, Our Innovators</strong></i></h6>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2 ;margin-top:0px; font-family:sans-serif;" class="description">At Pei Hwa Secondary School, innovation begins with our educators.</p>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2 ;margin-top:0px; font-family:sans-serif;" class="description">What started as a simple excel tool by Mr Kenneth Yeo (Subject Head, Personal &amp; Aestheic Development) to help track student progress grew into a school-wide dashboard used by all 88 teachers – saving each of them up to 60 hours of admin work per year.</p>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2 ;margin-top:0px; font-family:sans-serif;" class="description">With the support of Ms Shalini Tashnamurthu (HOD, Infocomm Tech), this homegrown system now consolidates MOE data, school records and post-secondary admission pathways – giving our teachers sharper insights to guide every student.</p>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2 ;margin-top:0px; font-family:sans-serif;" class="description">This passion project was recognized with the Minister’s Innovation Award.</p>

<p style="font-size:14.5px; line-height:2 ;margin-top:0px; font-family:sans-serif;" class="description">We are proud of our educators who aren’t just teaching – they are transforming the way we work and support students.</p>
